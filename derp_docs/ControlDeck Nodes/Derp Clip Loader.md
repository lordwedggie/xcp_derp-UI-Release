# <span style="color: #ff8080">Derp</span> <span style="color: #ffffff">CLIP Loader</span>

![image](../_assets/images/derpClipLoader_01.jpg)
The newest member of the loader family. Loads CLIP model files with proper weight and device settings, supporting 23 different CLIP architectures because the AI industry can't agree on anything.

<span style="color: #ffc680"><strong>Important:</strong></span> This loader needs [derpRouter](../Management%20Nodes/Derp%20Router.md) to function.

### <span style="color: #80ffc0">Features</span>

<span style="color: #80aaff"><strong>CLIP model deck</strong></span>: Load CLIP files onto the panel for one-click switching.

<span style="color: #80aaff"><strong>Refresh button</strong></span>: New CLIP models appeared? Click instead of restarting.

<span style="color: #80aaff"><strong>Clear button</strong></span>: Wipe the deck. Disabled when empty.

<span style="color: #80aaff"><strong>Remove button</strong></span>: Evict individual CLIP entries.

<span style="color: #80aaff"><strong>Drag and drop</strong></span>: Reorder your CLIP collection.

<span style="color: #80aaff"><strong>Search tab</strong></span>: Type to filter your CLIP list.

<span style="color: #80aaff"><strong>Settings toggle</strong></span>: The ⚙️ button in the header. Once your deck is loaded, toggle it off to hide the file browser and inline options for a cleaner look. Toggle back on when you need to add models or change CLIP type/device.

#### <span style="color: #80ffc0">Inline Options (visible when ⚙️ is on)</span>

<span style="color: #80aaff"><strong>CLIP Type</strong></span>: Select which architecture your CLIP model uses. 23 options from "stable_diffusion" to "ideogram4".

<span style="color: #80aaff"><strong>CLIP Device</strong></span>: Choose where to load — default (GPU) or cpu. Useful when VRAM is tight.

#### <span style="color: #80ffc0">System Panel Options</span>

<span style="color: #80aaff"><strong>Show Folder Names</strong></span>: Toggles folder paths in the display.
