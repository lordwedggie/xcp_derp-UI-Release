# <span style="color: #ff8080">Derp</span> <span style="color: #ffffff">Sampler Loader</span>

![image](../_assets/images/derpSamplerLoader_01.jpg)
Loads sampler names onto the node's deck so you can switch between them with a single click. Because life is too short to scroll through a dropdown every time you want to try Euler instead of DPM++ 2M.

<span style="color: #ffc680"><strong>Important:</strong></span> This loader needs [derpRouter](../Management%20Nodes/Derp%20Router.md) to function. Without it, it's just a very opinionated list of words.

### <span style="color: #80ffc0">Features</span>

<span style="color: #80aaff"><strong>Select Sampler dropdown</strong></span>: Browse and add samplers to your deck. Includes search so you don't have to squint at forty sampler names in 10pt font.

<span style="color: #80aaff"><strong>Refresh button</strong></span>: New samplers available? Click this instead of questioning your life choices.

<span style="color: #80aaff"><strong>Clear button</strong></span>: Wipe the entire deck clean. Starts grayed out until you've added at least one sampler, because pressing a button that does nothing is just sad.

<span style="color: #80aaff"><strong>Remove button</strong></span>: Evict an individual sampler from the deck. It appears next to the currently active sampler so you don't accidentally delete the wrong one.

<span style="color: #80aaff"><strong>Drag and drop</strong></span>: Reorder your samplers by dragging them around. Your workflow won't notice, but your OCD will thank you.
