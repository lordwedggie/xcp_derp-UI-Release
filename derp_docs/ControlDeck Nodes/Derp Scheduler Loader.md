# <span style="color: #ff8080">Derp</span> <span style="color: #ffffff">Scheduler Loader</span>

![image](../_assets/images/derpSchedulerLoader_01.jpg)
Loads scheduler names onto the node's deck for one-click switching. Because nothing says "I have my life together" like being able to jump from Normal to Karras without touching a dropdown.

<span style="color: #ffc680"><strong>Important:</strong></span> This loader needs [derpRouter](../Management%20Nodes/Derp%20Router.md) to function. Without it, it's just a list of scheduler names silently judging you.

### <span style="color: #80ffc0">Features</span>

<span style="color: #80aaff"><strong>Select Scheduler dropdown</strong></span>: Browse and add schedulers to your deck. Includes search because scrolling through Exponential, Karras, SGU, and their seventeen variants is nobody's idea of a good time.

<span style="color: #80aaff"><strong>Refresh button</strong></span>: New schedulers in your ComfyUI build? Click this instead of restarting.

<span style="color: #80aaff"><strong>Clear button</strong></span>: Nuke the whole deck. Grayed out when empty so you can't click a button that has nothing to do.

<span style="color: #80aaff"><strong>Remove button</strong></span>: Drop a single scheduler from the deck. Appears next to the active one so you don't accidentally yeet the wrong entry.

<span style="color: #80aaff"><strong>Drag and drop</strong></span>: Reorder your scheduler list. Does nothing functional, but neither do half the settings you tweak at 2am.
